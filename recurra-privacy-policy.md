# Recurra — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 14, 2026**

## Overview

Recurra is a bill and subscription tracking app that is designed with your privacy in mind. Your financial data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

## Data You Enter

All bills, subscriptions, and related information you add to Recurra are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

## Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics through PostHog to help us understand how the app is used and to improve the experience. This includes events such as which features are used and how often the app is opened. This data is not linked to your identity in any way — we do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information. No financial data you enter into the app is ever included in analytics.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- Names, email addresses, or contact information
- Financial account details, bank information, or payment data
- Location data
- Advertising identifiers

## Third-Party Services

Recurra uses PostHog for anonymous analytics. You can review their privacy policy at https://posthog.com/privacy. No other third-party services are used.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## Children's Privacy

Recurra is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at recurra.app@gmail.com.
